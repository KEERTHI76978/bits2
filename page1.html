Fish

Fish are diverse, cold-blooded, aquatic vertebrates with fins and gills that allow them to live underwater. They exist in a vast range of sizes and species, inhabiting both freshwater and saltwater environments worldwide. Key characteristics include a backbone, gills for breathing, fins for movement, and a variety of body coverings like scales.
